### Background

We need a new customer self-service portal to replace our aging customer support phone system. Customers should be able to manage their accounts online without calling in. This is a strategic priority — the board wants it live by Q3.

### What We Need

1. **Account Dashboard** — Customers log in and see their accounts, balances, and recent transactions.

2. **Fund Transfers** — Customers can transfer money between their own accounts and to other Contoso customers.

3. **Bill Pay** — Customers can set up and manage bill payments.

4. **Support Tickets** — Customers can submit support tickets and check status instead of calling.

5. **Document Upload** — Customers can upload documents we request (ID verification, proof of address, etc.).

6. **Notifications** — Customers get notified about important account activity.

7. **Profile Management** — Customers can update their contact info and preferences.

### Technical Notes

- Should work on phones and desktops
- Needs to be secure (we're a bank)
- Should integrate with our existing core banking API
- The marketing team wants it to look modern

### Timeline

- Phase 1 by end of Q3 2026
- Full launch by Q4 2026
